Structured and Simultaneous Lyapunov Functions for System Stability Problems

S. Boyd and Q. Yang

International Journal of Control, 49(6):2215-2240, 1989.
Also appeared in Robustness in Identification and Control, M. Milanese, R. Tempo, and A. Vicino, editors, Plenum Press, pp.243-262, 1989.

  • IJC article: simul_lyapunov.pdf

It is shown that many system stability and robustness problems can be reduced to the question of when there is a quadratic Lyapunov function of a certain structure which establishes stability of dx/dt=Ax for some appropriate A. The existence of such a Lyapunov function can be determined by solving a convex program. We present several numerical methods for these optimization problems. A simple numerical example is given.
